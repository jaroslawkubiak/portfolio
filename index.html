About me

Hello. My name is Jarosław. After graduation from the Kazimierza Wielkiego University in Bydgoszcz, I got freelance job to write software for window production company from my region. At first it was a very simple website, on which user could log in to the system, add customers profiles and their orders to the databese, view and edit them. My engineering thesis for academic credit was to write a warehouse management software in PHP and MySQL, therefore, the programme I was commissioned for also uses the same technologies. That project has developed over 7 years and ended in 2014. A new company was established in 2015, by former employees of the previous company and I get further order for a similar project. This project is still being developed to the present day, new functionalities are being added to improve my client's work. For these few years this simple project has grown into quite big ERP system (more than 60,000 lines of code). You can find out more about this system in my portfolio.
